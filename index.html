Important Details

Welcome to our wedding website! Thank you for being a part of our celebration. This is the place you'll find every piece of information you will need regarding our wedding celebration. This will be updated regularly.


Our wedding will be taking place in beautiful Indore, Madhya Pradesh on January 8, 2018. The celebrations will begin from January 6, 2018. Nikita was born and brought up in Indore and we couldn't think of a better place to celebrate this special occasion than the place where she celebrated 20 years of her life. So mark your calendars because we would love for you to come and celebrate with us!


We look forward to celebrating our wedding with as many friends and family members as possible, but we realize that a trip to Indore is not a possibility for some. For those who cannot make the trip to Indore, we will have a celebration in Mumbai, Maharashtra following the wedding on January 13, 2018 so that we can share our joy with all of you.

Sweet love story

August 2013

We first meet in the fall of 2013 in Atlanta, Georgia. It seems like yesterday when he saw an ambitious girl sitting in front of him in the Crypto class at Georgia Tech. Mutual friends introduced us and soon we became project partners. During the difficult time of struggling between classes, project deadlines and adjusting in a new Country, our friend grew stronger. Little did we know that was the beginning of our forever lasting friendship. We went from project partners to friends to best friends and today we are getting married. We can not feel more grateful that everything came together for us and with the blessings from both the families we are about to take the 7 auspicious vows in the presence of the sacred fire. Although our journey may not have began like a fairy tale, We know we are destined for a story book ending.
